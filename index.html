FAQ

Common Questions

What format do the templates come in?
All templates are delivered as Google Sheets (fully editable, no software required) plus Notion templates where applicable. PDF guides are included with each kit. Everything is delivered instantly via Gumroad download immediately after purchase.
Which payment platforms does the audit cover?
The Payout Audit Kit and Reconciliation Pack cover Stripe, PayPal, Gumroad, Square, Shopify Payments, Wise (TransferWise), and ACH bank transfers. Platform-by-platform reconciliation guides are included for each, along with fee calculators that reflect current platform rates.
Do I need accounting experience to use these?
No. Every template is built for agency owners, not accountants. Each spreadsheet includes instructions, and each kit includes a step-by-step guide explaining exactly what to do and in what order. If you can read a bank statement, you can run a full payout audit.
What's the difference between the Complete System and the Accelerator Bundle?
The Accelerator Bundle ($197) combines the Audit Kit, Dashboard Templates, and Reconciliation Pack — the three core operational tools — at a bundled price with a 30-day implementation roadmap. The Complete FinOps Management System ($97) is a single all-in-one product that includes those tools plus annual budgeting, tax prep, contractor tracking, and KPI scorecards. The Complete System is the better single-purchase value; the Bundle is ideal if you want the individual products with implementation guidance.
What does the DFY audit include, and how does it work?
After purchase, you'll complete a short intake form and share your last 90 days of payout reports from your payment platforms (via a secure file share). We analyze them for discrepancies, fee errors, timing issues, and missed recovery opportunities, then deliver a custom Profit Leak Report with a prioritized action plan within 14 business days. We also set up a custom financial dashboard pre-loaded with your business structure. For ongoing management, the Monthly FinOps Retainer is available after the DFY engagement.
